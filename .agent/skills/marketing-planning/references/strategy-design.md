# Strategy Design

## Core Frameworks

Apply these strategic frameworks:
- **RACE** - Reach, Act, Convert, Engage
- **SOSTAC** - Situation, Objectives, Strategy, Tactics, Actions, Control
- **STP** - Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning
- **SMART Goals** - Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound

## Design Activities

### Positioning Strategy
- Define unique value proposition
- Craft positioning statement
- Identify competitive differentiation
- Develop key messaging pillars
- Create message hierarchy

### Audience Strategy
- Define primary and secondary segments
- Create detailed buyer personas
- Map customer journey stages
- Identify trigger moments and pain points
- Plan personalization approach

### Channel Strategy
- Select priority marketing channels
- Define channel-specific tactics
- Plan content distribution
- Allocate budget across channels
- Set channel-specific KPIs

### Campaign Architecture
- Design campaign structure and phases
- Plan content calendar and cadence
- Create offer and promotion strategy
- Design conversion funnels
- Plan A/B testing approach

### Budget & Resource Planning
- Allocate budget by channel and tactic
- Identify resource requirements
- Plan agency/freelancer needs
- Set ROI expectations
- Plan contingency reserves

### Risk Assessment
- Identify potential campaign risks
- Consider competitive responses
- Plan for underperformance scenarios
- Design pivot strategies
- Consider compliance and legal risks

## Best Practices

- Document strategic decisions and rationale
- Balance brand building with performance
- Think through entire customer journey
- Plan for measurement and optimization
- Design with testing in mind
