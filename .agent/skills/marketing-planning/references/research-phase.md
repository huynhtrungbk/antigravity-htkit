# Market Research Phase

**When to skip:** If provided with market research reports, skip this phase.

## Core Activities

### Parallel Research Agents
- Spawn multiple `marketing-research` agents to investigate different market aspects
- Wait for all agents to report back before proceeding
- Each researcher covers: competitors, audience, trends, benchmarks

### Market Analysis
- Use `marketing-research` skill for comprehensive market intelligence
- Analyze market size, growth trends, and opportunities
- Identify market gaps and positioning opportunities

### Competitor Research
- Analyze competitor positioning and messaging
- Review competitor content strategies and campaigns
- Benchmark pricing, features, and offers

### Audience Insights
- Define target audience segments and personas
- Research audience pain points and motivations
- Identify audience channels and touchpoints
- Map customer journey stages

### Trend Analysis
- Research industry trends and emerging opportunities
- Identify seasonal patterns and timing considerations
- Monitor social listening for sentiment and topics

## Best Practices

- Research breadth before depth
- Document findings for synthesis phase
- Identify multiple positioning options
- Consider competitive differentiation early
- Note regulatory or compliance considerations
